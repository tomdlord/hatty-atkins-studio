Products to brighten up your home

A collection of contemporary homeware products, made in clay, jesmonite and plaster. Inspired by our interaction with colour, shape and function within the home.

Collection

Modu

Colourful Jesmonite homeware products. This collection of modular homeware products are all hand cast in small batches. The collection compromises of a Jewellery dish, ring cone, watch rest, incense holder and candle holder. The colour palette has been specifically designed and hand mixed for the range inspired by how we interact with certain colours, shape and function within the home.
